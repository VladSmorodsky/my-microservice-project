Django app deployed as release "{{ .Release.Name }}".

1. Wait for the LoadBalancer to get an external address:

   kubectl get svc {{ include "django-app.fullname" . }} -w

2. Open the app (use the EXTERNAL-IP / hostname shown above):

   http://<EXTERNAL-IP>:{{ .Values.service.port }}/

3. Inspect autoscaling (requires metrics-server in the cluster):

   kubectl get hpa {{ include "django-app.fullname" . }}
   kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}
